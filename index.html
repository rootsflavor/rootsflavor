01 — About Us०१ — हाम्रो बारेमा

Rooted in Nepal's Highlandsनेपालका पहाडमा जरा

The story of Roots Flavorरुट्स फ्लेभरको कथा

Roots Flavor was born from a simple belief — that Nepal's highland farmers produce extraordinary grains and pulses that deserve to reach more tables. We started in Dolpa, one of Nepal's most remote and pristine districts, where generations of farmers have cultivated crops the traditional way.रुट्स फ्लेभर एउटा सरल विश्वासबाट जन्मियो — नेपालका पहाडी किसानहरूले असाधारण अन्न र दलहन उत्पादन गर्छन् जुन थप टेबलमा पुग्नु पर्छ। हामी डोल्पाबाट सुरु भयौं।

We work directly with farmers in Dolpa, cutting out unnecessary middlemen and ensuring fair prices at both ends — for the farmers who grow, and the agri businesses who buy.हामी डोल्पाका किसानहरूसँग सिधै काम गर्छौं, अनावश्यक बिचौलियाहरूलाई हटाएर दुवैतर्फ उचित मूल्य सुनिश्चित गर्दै।

Based in Kathmandu, with deep roots in Dolpa, we are building Nepal's most trusted highland agri supply chain — one honest transaction at a time.काठमाडौंमा आधारित, डोल्पामा गहिरो जडसहित, हामी नेपालको सबैभन्दा भरपर्दो हाइल्याण्ड कृषि आपूर्ति श्रृंखला निर्माण गर्दैछौं।
